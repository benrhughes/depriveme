I've been experimenting with taking 24hr breaks from the things in my life that I enjoy or depend on. It's a great way to appreciate what I have, and to learn how to live without it.
DepriveMe helps me organise and randomise my days of self deprivation. It works like this:
  • You enter a list of things you'd like to take a break from. Mine includes: coffee; food; hot showers; my smartphone and music
  • You specify how many days you'd want the list spread across
  • If it doesn't already exist, a 'DepriveMe' calendar is created in your Google Calandar
  • Each item is randomly distributed across the time period
