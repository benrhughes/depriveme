For a while I've been experimenting with taking a 24hr break from the things in my life that I enjoy or depend on. I've found that it's a great way to increase my appreciation of what I have. Plus it helps me lessen my attachment to material things.

DepriveMe is a simple tool I wrote to help me keep this organised. Randomising it makes it a bit more fun, too. It works like this:
  • You enter a list of things you'd like to take a scheduled break from. Mine includes: coffee; food; hot showers; my smartphone and music
  • You specify how long a period you want the list spread across
  • If it doesn't already exist, a 'DepriveMe' calendar is created in your Google Calandar
  • DepriveMe randomly distributes each item across the time period
